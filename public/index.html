...where you can belong to a school club, a group of gamers, or a global artistic community. Where you and some friends can spend time together. A place that makes it easy to chat every day and socialize more often.

    Create an invite controlled space where you feel at home

    Discord servers are organized into channels with topics for you to collaborate, share, or simply talk about your day without clogging up a general chat

    It's easy to meet up here

    Hop into a voice channel when you're free. Friends on the same server can see you and join the channel without an invite

    For a few or a crowd

    Organize any community with moderation tools and personalized member access. Give special powers to members, create private channels, and more
